## Types of Storage
- **HDD:** Mechanical, slower, cheaper
- **SSD:** Fast, no moving parts
- **NVMe:** Very fast, uses PCIe lanes

## Form Factors
| Type | Form Factor | Interface |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| HDD | 3.5", 2.5" | SATA |
| SSD | 2.5" | SATA |
| SSD | M.2 | SATA or NVMe |

## RAID Levels
- RAID 0: Striping (performance)
- RAID 1: Mirroring (redundancy)
- RAID 5: Striping + parity
- RAID 10: Mirror + stripe
